Spring 2021 Recruitment - Graphic Design & Digital Marketing

January 4th, 2021

UBC AgroBot is looking for members interested in marketing, media, and graphic design. You will get the opportunity to combine design, creativity, and business to help AgroBot promote our exciting projects for a future-proof way of farming. You will be collaborating with our different sub-teams to communicate our project to a wide range of audiences through different platforms.

Applications will be considered on a rolling basis until positions are filled. Apply today to join our team for 2021 Spring!

Responsibilities:
-Generate design ideas and create visuals by communicating with members in the technical projects
-Working with the Sponsorship & Industry Relations Team to design sponsorship packages or other promotional materials (email, ads, posters, videos)
-Maintaining social media platforms (FB, LinkedIn, Instagram)
-Supporting web development with design ideas and graphics
-Using design software (Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, etc.)
-Approximate 3 hours a week (time is very flexible; it depends on your level of experience)
