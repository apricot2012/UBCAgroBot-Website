End of Year Announcement

May 12th, 2020

This year has been a meaningful and successful debut for AgroBot!

We have worked hard throughout the year to build our very first agricultural robot prototype, from designing, procuring parts and building the various systems.

Some of our highlights for the 2019/2020 school year include:

  • Becoming an official UBC engineering design team and recruiting 32 students from various disciplines
  • Building a mobile chassis, omnidirectional nozzle, as well as a weed recognition and navigation system

We would like to thank everyone who has supported us on our journey, and we will continue to work remotely on the project throughout the summer. We hope that with the current circumstances we are in that everyone stays safe.

Warm regards,
UBC AgroBot
